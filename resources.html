Helpful Resources

Find links and information to support your journey. We've compiled a list of resources that can help you share your story and find support.

Mental Health Support

  • World Health Organization (WHO) Mental Health

    Information and resources on mental health from the WHO.

  • National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI)

    Support, education, and advocacy for individuals and families affected by mental illness.

  • Crisis Text Line

    Free, 24/7 support for those in crisis. Text HOME to 741741 from anywhere in the US, anytime, about any type of crisis.

  • The Trevor Project

    Crisis intervention and suicide prevention services for LGBTQ young people under 25.

Writing and Storytelling Resources

  • Writer's Digest

    Articles, tips, and resources for writers of all levels.

  • Creative Writing Now

    Creative writing prompts, exercises, and tips to inspire your writing.

  • StoryCorps

    An archive of human stories and resources for recording your own.

  • Grammarly Blog

    Tips for writing, grammar, and style.

Support Groups and Communities

  • SupportGroups.com

    Find online and in-person support groups for various issues.

  • Meetup

    Find local groups and communities based on your interests.

  • 7 Cups

    Online emotional support and counseling.
